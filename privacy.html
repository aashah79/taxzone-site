Privacy Policy

Your privacy and the confidentiality of your information are our highest priorities

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Income Tax & Bookkeeping, Inc. (doing business as TaxZone.ai) is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our clients' personal and financial information. As a professional tax preparation service, we adhere to the highest standards of data protection and confidentiality required by law and professional ethics.

Information We Collect

In order to provide professional tax preparation and bookkeeping services, we collect and maintain the following types of information:

Personal Information:

  • Name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Social Security numbers and dates of birth
  • Family and dependent information
  • Banking and financial account information

Financial Information:

  • Income information (W-2s, 1099s, business income)
  • Tax deductions and credits
  • Investment and retirement account information
  • Business financial records and transactions
  • Prior year tax returns

Communication Information:

  • Email correspondence and phone call records
  • Meeting notes and consultation records
  • Service preferences and requests

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for legitimate business purposes related to providing tax preparation and bookkeeping services:

  • Preparing and filing your tax returns
  • Providing bookkeeping and accounting services
  • Communicating about your account and services
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Defending against potential claims or audits
  • Improving our services and operations

🔒 What We DON'T Do

  • We do NOT sell your information to marketers or third parties
  • We do NOT share your information for marketing purposes
  • We do NOT use your information for our own marketing campaigns
  • We do NOT provide lists of client information to anyone

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We maintain strict confidentiality and only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

With Your Written Consent:

  • When you specifically authorize us to share information with third parties
  • To communicate with your other professional advisors (accountants, lawyers, financial planners)

Required by Law:

  • IRS and state tax authorities (for filing returns and responding to inquiries)
  • Court orders and legal subpoenas
  • Professional oversight and regulatory bodies

Service Providers:

  • Professional tax software providers (with appropriate security agreements)
  • Electronic filing services
  • Banking institutions for payment processing
  • Professional backup and storage services (encrypted and secured)

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access on a need-to-know basis only
  • Professional Software: Bank-level security in all tax preparation systems
  • Secure Storage: Physical documents stored in locked, fireproof cabinets
  • Regular Updates: Security software and protocols updated regularly
  • Staff Training: All team members trained on confidentiality and data protection

Communication and Marketing

We may communicate with you for service-related purposes:

  • Appointment reminders and service updates
  • Important tax law changes that may affect you
  • Annual tax season reminders
  • Invoice notifications and payment reminders

📱 Text Message Communications

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive text messages from TaxZone.ai for service-related communications. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of text messages, reply STOP to any text message you receive from us.

For support, reply HELP or contact us at 708-394-SAVE.

Data Retention

We retain your information in accordance with professional standards and legal requirements:

  • Active Records: Maintained while you are an active client
  • Tax Returns: Retained for a minimum of 7 years after filing
  • Supporting Documents: Retained for 7 years or as required by law
  • Business Records: Retained according to applicable regulations
  • Secure Disposal: All information securely destroyed after retention period expires

Your Rights

As our client, you have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request copies of your information in our files
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Explanation: Receive explanation of how we use and protect your information
  • Limitation: Request limitations on how we use or share your information
  • Complaint: File complaints about our information handling practices

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, except as necessary to prepare family tax returns with parental consent and involvement.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify clients of material changes by email or written notice.
