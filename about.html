Our Story

A legacy of trust, integrity, and service spanning over five decades

Honoring Our Founder

Arvind Shah (1944-2026)

Founder & Visionary

Born in Kakanpur, India, in 1944, Arvind Shah embodied the American dream. After working in Africa as a young man, he immigrated to the United States in 1972 with nothing but determination and strong values.

Like many immigrants, he entered a completely unfamiliar country and built his life step by step through hard work and courage — never complaining, just moving forward. Everything he did was for family and community.

Professionally, Arvind worked for decades as a tax accountant, known for his honesty, trustworthiness, and genuine care for others. He earned the respect and loyalty of clients who valued not only his expertise but also his kindness and character.

He was also actively involved in the Khadayata Samaj community, serving multiple terms as president and supporting cultural and community initiatives.

What defined Arvind wasn't just his professional success — it was his character. He chose to protect others over himself, believed in doing the right thing, and wanted everyone to feel valued. He had an unwavering belief in himself and used his strength to help others succeed.

Above all, Arvind was devoted to his family. He found his greatest joy in spending time with his wife, children, and grandchildren. He enjoyed family gatherings, good food, dancing, bowling, and cheering on his grandchildren in their activities.

"Hope for the best. Prepare for the worst." — Words he lived by, facing every challenge with strength and determination.

His legacy lives on through the values he instilled: integrity, hard work, genuine care for others, and always putting family first.
