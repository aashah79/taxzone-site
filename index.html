About Our Founder

Arvind Shah (1944-2026)

Our founder immigrated from India in 1972 and built his life through hard work, determination, and unwavering integrity. He served the Des Plaines community as a trusted tax accountant for decades, known for his honesty and genuine care for others.

Arvind believed in doing the right thing, protecting others, and helping everyone feel valued. His legacy lives on through the values he instilled: integrity, hard work, and always putting family and community first.
