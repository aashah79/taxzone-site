Individual Tax Preparation

Expert preparation of personal income tax returns with meticulous attention to every deduction and credit you deserve.

What's Included:

  • Complete federal and state tax return preparation
  • Maximum deduction and credit optimization
  • Prior year amendments (when beneficial)
  • Electronic filing with direct deposit/withdrawal options
  • Tax planning consultation for next year
  • Audit support and representation

Perfect For:

  • W-2 employees with complex deductions
  • Homeowners maximizing mortgage interest and property tax deductions
  • Families with children (Child Tax Credits, Education Credits)
  • Retirees managing Social Security and retirement distributions
  • Anyone with investment income or capital gains/losses

Business Tax Services

Comprehensive business tax preparation and planning for all entity types, from sole proprietorships to complex corporations.

Entity Types We Serve:

  • Sole Proprietorships (Schedule C)
  • Partnerships (Form 1065)
  • S Corporations (Form 1120S)
  • C Corporations (Form 1120)
  • LLCs (All tax elections)
  • Non-Profits (Form 990 series)

Our Business Services:

  • Federal and state business tax return preparation
  • Quarterly estimated tax planning and payments
  • Payroll tax returns and compliance
  • Sales tax return preparation and filing
  • Entity formation and tax election guidance
  • Multi-state tax compliance
  • Business tax planning and strategy
